Fix broken cable tip stuck inside your IAXE629

Last Updated on Monday, 09 January 2012 15:08 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1246

How to fix the broken 6.35mm cable tip that stuck inside your Behringer IAXE-629 guitar? Disassemble guide and instruction of my own annoying experience ...

This was just happened after I bought my IAXE-629 USB Guitar for a few day. The cable tip had broken and stuck inside the phono unit sitting next to the USB interface ... panic attack!

When this is happened (DO and DON'T)

  • It is very important to pull yourself together. Don't panic (easily said than done, happen to me too). The problem could be solve reasonably easily. Grab your self a cup of tea could help get rid of some frustration. :)
  • DON'T try to use any sticking substance to pull the tip off. I found that some people suggested using the super glue or blue tag (It will be very lucky if it works first try). I tried them too, ended up worse not even the broken tip still stuck and also making a mess and have to clean up those sticking glue from the contact switch :(

Disassemble the unit

The picture showed the tool I used. M0, M1 screw driver is very handy basic tool for working on the small electronic part. Cotton butt, electronic squeezer and the cleaning spray for cleaning my earlier glue mess. I got them from Maplin store. You should be able to find it from any electronic joint around your corner thou :)

Unscrew the faceplate

Carefully remove the metal plate with the USB PCB board until you see that small wire. (fig.3) Carefully remove the terminal using the M0. You might have to remove the glue from the terminal if needed.

Carefully unscrew (4 of them) the metal plate to separate the PCB from it.

Removing that little devil

Now we have to open the mounted connector (I don't know what is this black box called and will be very appreciated if anyone could tell me its name and where could I buy the spare part), find the broken tip and take it out.

Have to be very careful here. From the Fig.6 Using the small M0 (flat one) to open the closed cover box.

Slide them apart (Fig. 7), while taking a good care of the 'small leg' (Fig.8). Again, I don't know what is this black box called and will be very appreciated if anyone could tell me its name and where could I buy this spare part.

Here finally you can see the bare connector plate. Now you can remove the broken tip quite easily from the part. :) You also can clean up the connector using the cleaning spray here too!

Reassemble the unit carefully by reversing all those previous steps. Now you can do one less annoying problem on your IAXE-629. Happy practicing your guitar. hooray!
