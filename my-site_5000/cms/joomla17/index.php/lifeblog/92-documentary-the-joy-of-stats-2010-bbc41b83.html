Documentary: The Joy of Stats (2010), BBC4

Last Updated on Sunday, 15 January 2012 22:41 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1024

The first time in several years that statistics make me smile. The name of the show is simply self-explained. The Joy of Stats, is the documentary about Statistics and Data analysis. The topic itself sounds boring and uninteresting, but wait. Prof. Rosling is here to show you that it is not always true!

Prof. Hans Rosling has proved in this programme where the statistic lecture could be very fun and lots less boring with his alternative explanation and interactive presentation. I admitted that even I love Maths, but statistic is always one big pain in the butt. After the show, I honestly accepted myself a better feeling about the whole subject. Thanks to the show that leave me this good feeling which could encourage me to dig up some of my old books and reading them again.
