Film: The Prestige (2006)

Last Updated on Thursday, 22 December 2011 09:27 | | Hits: 714

Do you like magic? The prestige, is the film that told the story between two magicians which somehow become rivals where each of them want to become the best and stay ahead of each others. The film itself is very awesome and very enjoy to watch. It has a very good blend among Drama, Thriller and Mystery. Not only that, since what has caught me the most is how the movie also tries to link the mystery of classic science discovery with the world of magician so seamlessly.

10/10, go watch it.
