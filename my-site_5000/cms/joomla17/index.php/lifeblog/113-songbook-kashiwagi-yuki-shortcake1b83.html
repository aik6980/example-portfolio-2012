Songbook: Kashiwagi Yuki – Shortcake

Last Updated on Saturday, 22 February 2014 11:21 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1313

[Songbook] is a collection of some of my favorite, nicely touch piece of music where I try to transcribe their chords, and learn to play by myself. It simply started because I was so frustrated that Google cannot find chords for the song I want to play, so I decided to do it myself and decided also to share them here.

Kashiwagi Yuki is a member of the famous Japanese girl group AKB48. Here, I would like to share a nice lovely song from her solo album with the same name "Shortcake".

My favorite moment: 
I really love the 2/3/4/5 sequence in the chorus and the flavour of maj7 chords :)
