Documentary: Last One Standing (2007)

Last Updated on Monday, 16 January 2012 08:34 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1500

First world athlete training VS Nature survivor tribal warrior, the documentary will bring you the answer while the 6 six young brilliant athletes will be competed against tribe warriors on their traditional tribal sports along their trip around the world

Prepare for full contact culture with diverse athletes, remote tribes and real competition!

Follow six courageous athletes who live and train with the world’s most remote indigenous tribes in a raw and intense array of tribal games and rite of passage ceremonies. From death-defying Zulu stick fighting in South Africa to an arduous foot race in the Mexican mountains, watch these men push their physical and mental limits to see who will be the last warrior standing.

This is more than just a physical competition, the story is also about these men's life, their goal and ambition which has been revealed through their expression and experience they have been along these somewhat spiritual journey.
