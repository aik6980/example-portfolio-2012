Rapunzel, the Mockingbird

Last Updated on Friday, 13 January 2012 23:29 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1270

Rapunzel is the name I called my guitar, BC Rich MockingBird ProX 2011. The name come from one of my favorite Disney princess, Rapunzel, from Tangled.

She has not only have a noble look, slick and beautiful of black unfinished mahogany, but also quite fragile and quite easy to leave a scratch if you are not careful enough. It surely does feel like taking care of a beautiful noble girl, a princess.    

Inspired by my favorite guitarist, HIDE, the Mockingbird has become my favorite guitar since I have remembered. However, I have to wait for more than 10 years until I am ready to buy one myself.  

I used the Focusrite Scarlett 2i2, paired with KRK Monitors to help me recording my work. This gives me quite an accurate sound, and it somehow does make I feel safe to have the monitor system I can rely on.
