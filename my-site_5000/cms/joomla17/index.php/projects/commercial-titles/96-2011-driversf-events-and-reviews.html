2011

E3 2011

Rounding up from E3 2011, it has been all gloriously fun as far as I have been told. Thanks for everyone who has been supporting Driver: San Francisco and given us a very good feedback! And finally the project is getting very close to the release.
This good fun driving game will be scheduled on the shelf in September! Armed with a lot of Cops&Robbers chases with Shift machanic for a big twist!
