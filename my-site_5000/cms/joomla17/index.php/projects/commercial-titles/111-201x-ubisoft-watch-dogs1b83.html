Watch Dogs, is the next-gen open world game that will give players a new form of freedom aiming for a fully immersive gameplay. This is clearly one of the most ambitious project in its own rights, both gameplay and graphics (especially graphics). The biggest collaboration among so many studios I have ever participated.

Roles and responsibilities

I worked as a graphic programmer on various VFX and Post Processing.

One of the coolest thing about this project is that we are aiming for a very cutting edge graphics, therefore, as a graphic engineer, there is a lot of freedom where you are allowed to try experimenting with so many graphic techniques which is very much fun.
