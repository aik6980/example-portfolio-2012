I have joined the Driver project in 2009, as a rendering/graphic programmer.

Roles and responsibilities

- Daily job is to maintain the graphic performance and stability of our 60Hz game engine, monitoring changes, fixing bugs. (GPAD and PIX as a regular basis)

- Create and maintain a number of VFX that you can see from the final product, working in collaboration with the Art Director and Artists.
- Analyzing the requests before turn them into an effective GPU program, and make sure it will fit into the game (performance and memory-wise) regarding to this 60Hz mandatory.

Driver:SF Overview

Tanner is back! Driver:SF is coming back on the street, with the great will to head back to the form as the famous original. The game features the huge lively iconic city, with more than 200 miles of road for an utimate chase, 100+ licenced cars, and smooth car control with the fluid gameplay!
