Equilibrium (2010)

Last Updated on Friday, 13 January 2012 20:30 | Written by Super User | | Hits: 1858

Equilibrium is an RTS game building for a mobile platform. I have worked on this small project on my spare time as a graphic programmer. In 2010, this could be one of the best thing I have ever spent my weekend time on.

It all has been started somewhere in 2009, one of my best friend, Equilibrium Creative Director, who also my respectful programmer had managed to gathered a few talented artists where they all shared the same dream, the dream of building a fun RTS game.

They have told me about their need of a graphic programmer at the time. It doesn't take long to decide, as I really want to help them laying out the ground work of the rendering system during the protoype phase. 

I have so much fun and learnt a lot from the project, the OpenTK API, the PowerVR SDK, Mobile phone emulators, game system design and especially graphic capability restrictions on the Android device.

However, as we all know, the fun couldn't last forever. Once the non-commercial, open-source prototype is finished, I have decided to leave the project in order to avoid the problem of publicher's work complication.

Yet the most important thing, since I believed is a wonderful game, this will allow my friend to put the next version of Equilibrium on to market, and find himself the real funding for it.

 

Time flies ...

 

Today is 2012, it has been a while. I'm writing this article with full of heartiness that my work would have been useful to them as much as it is benefitted me replinishing new knowledges. 
I wish all my friends for their brightest future and I am sure I will see the game in the full retail someday.
