May 16, 2008 : Group Project Showcase

It's the last day of the 3rd year student, the year that's quite hassle and busy.

Just give a second to stop and think about what we had been going through in the project. Everything was happened so fast, lacking of game software management experience caused some of the hassle and misplans.
Anyway, at the end we still manage to hold our spirit as the team, and finish all the jobs in the proper timeframe. The game looks lovely. It's completed as best the way it is, even there're still some minor bugs .
Working in the inexperience team gave me back many interesting issues about team in game development and this is definitely some piece of valuable experience that I will keep and concern into my next project.

Thanks to all the members to whom we might work together again, and whom I might not.
